HOW TO PLAY:
All colours are a mix of red, green and blue. In the world of computers, these mixes can be represented as three numbers: an amount of red (0-255), an amount of green (0-255) and an amount of green (0-255).
Use the numbers provided and try to guess which colour the mix generates.
